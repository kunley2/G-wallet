Questions

about Salte

What is Salte ?
Salte is a cutting-edge facial recognition payment app that allows you to make secure and convenient transactions using your facial biometrics.
How does Salte work ?
Salte uses advanced facial recognition technology to securely capture and analyze your unique facial features. When making a payment, your face is scanned to authorize the transaction, ensuring a seamless and safe experience.
Is Salte secure ?
Yes, Salte prioritizes security above all else. Our app uses encrypted data transmission and facial recognition algorithms to protect your personal information and prevent unauthorized access.
What devices are compatible with Salte ?
Salte is compatible with most modern smartphones and tablets that have a front-facing camera and meet our minimum system requirements. Please check our website for the full list of supported devices.
Can someone use a photo of me to make payment ?
Rest assured, Salte incorporates robust liveliness detection technology to prevent the use of photos for unauthorized payments.Our facial recognition system is designed to differentiate between live, 3d facial features and static images or videos. When making a payment, Salte actively verifies the presence of liveliness cues, such as subtle movements and depth, to ensure that the face being scanned is genuinely live and not a mere photo or video.
Does Salte store user's facial biometric data ?
Our system converts your facial features into a unique mathematical representation called a hash. This process is irreversible, meaning that the original facial image cannot be reconstructed from the stored data.
